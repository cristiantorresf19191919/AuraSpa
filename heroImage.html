Massage Therapy

Our therapists pride themselves in providing individualized massage sessions catered to the needs of each client. However, choosing from one of our services below helps the therapist know your goals of your session. Prices listed exclude Injury PIP, Medical, Auto Accident, and Workman's Comp Recovery Massage

Swedish Massage

$55+

Massage oil or cream is used with smooth, gliding strokes. Swedish massage uses firm but gentle pressure to promote relaxation, ease muscle tension and create other health benefits. It is a classic massage that will ease your stress and allow you to slip into a calm state of relaxation. Choose from a 30, 60, or 90 minute session. 

Deep Tissue/Therapeutic Massage

$55+

Designed to remove severe tension, deep tissue massage is a technique used to relieve both muscles and connective tissue below the surface. It helps to improve range of motion and heal injuries below the surface. Choose from a 30, 60, or a 90 minute session.

Auto Accident/Workmans Comp Recovery

Rx Required

Have you recently been in an auto accident or injured at work? We can help! We work with auto and workman's comp insurance claims so the benefits of massage are available to you at little or no cost to you. A doctor's script and a claim number from your insurance company are required. Call us today to learn more and set up an appointment.

Prenatal Massage

$55+

Prenatal massage focuses on addressing the special needs of expecting mothers. Specially-designed massage tables or cushions, or side-lying postures are used to keep the expecting mother comfortable while preventing putting pressure on the growing baby. Choose from a 30 or 60 minute session.

Full Body Salt Scrub

$120

This invigorating treatment uses a mineral sea salt scrub on the entire body in preparation to receive the maximum benefits from your massage. A combination of exfoliating salts and essential oils are used to smooth the skin, relax stressed muscles, and soothe the senses into a deep state of relaxation.

Mud Wrap

$120

Say goodbye to excess water, impurities, and dull pores with a layer of therapeutic mud that is lightly massaged into your skin before wrapping in order to detoxify, tone, and firm your body from head-to-toe.

Cupping Massage

$55+

Massage Cupping is a modern adaptation of the ancient art of cupping therapy. While massage performed by applying pressure, cupping instead uses negative pressure to pull, lift, open and expand the body’s soft tissues. Choose from a 30, 60, or 90 minute session.

Myofasical Release

$45+

Myofascial release is a soft tissue therapy for the treatment of skeletal muscle immobility and pain. The method engages the myofascial tissue and loads it with constant force until release occurs. To achieve this, practitioners can use knuckles, elbows, or other tools to slowly stretch the restricted fascia. The practitioner moves slowly through the layers of the fascia until the deep tissues are reached. Choose from a 30, 60, or 90 minute session.

Couples Massage

$190+

Experience all the wonderful benefits of massage with someone you love, whether it be a sister, friend, significant other, or spouse! This session is designed for two people to receive a massage at the same time, in the same private room, but on two separate massage tables and each person with their own therapist. To schedule a couple's massage please call.

Chair Massage

$25+

Chair Massage is performed in a specially designed chair with your clothes on. It is excellent for relaxation and more, depending on the technique(s) used by your massage therapist. Choose from a 15 or 30 min session.

Reiki

$45+

Reiki is a gentle touch therapy based on an Eastern belief that vital energy flows through the body. The practitioner uses a gentle touch or places hands just above the body – to help guide this energy in a way that promotes balance and healing.

Reiki was originally developed by a Japanese man named Mikao Usui in the 1920s. The term comes from two Japanese words: “Rei,” which means universal, and “ki,” which loosely translates to a lifeforce of energy. Only available with Mary.
